## Scope

The checklist covers planning, application preparation, listing and landlord verification, unit inspection, screening, lease review, deposits, disclosures, habitability, utilities, move-in evidence, repairs, fair-housing assistance and move-out.

Location overlays add only supplemental tasks. The composed API preserves `source_layer`, `source_location`, conditions and authoritative references.

## Key California checks

- Current tenant guidance and applicable disclosures.
- Application screening fee, receipt, report and unused-fee handling.
- Reusable tenant screening report availability.
- Security-deposit limits, claimed exceptions, permitted uses and evidence.
- Habitability, locks, smoke alarms and carbon-monoxide alarms.
- Applicability of state rent-increase and just-cause protections.
- Written repair requests and evidence preservation.
- Initial move-out inspection and deposit accounting.

## San Joaquin County checks

- San Joaquin Fair Housing tenant education, assistance and rental resources.
- Independent verification of every listing before payment.
- Correct city or county complaint channel for unsafe or environmental conditions.
- Address-specific flood-risk review.

## Manteca checks

- Inspect the actual property and verify the authorized landlord or manager.
- Look for visible code, utility, safety and unpermitted-conversion concerns.
- Confirm whether the tenant must open City water, sewer and solid-waste service.
- Prepare the City move-in application, government ID, lease and current deposit.
- Put utility allocation and shared-billing terms in the lease.
- Learn local cart and collection rules.
- Route private landlord disputes to fair-housing or legal resources; route covered physical-code violations to Code Enforcement.
- Stop tenant-held City utility service and retain the final bill at move-out.
